Journey

Day 1

Depart from KLIA/ changi airport to Milan Malpensa airport

Day 2

Milan City Tour

 Visit Milan Duomo and the glass domed Galleria mall

 ​​The Romantic Gondola Ride (Optional)

 ​​​Indulge in a Gondola ride cutting through the canals and get a glimpse of life of the ordinary Venetian on the Islands

 ​Return to Hotel Milan

Day 3

Venice Island

 ​Heading to the ever-happening San Marco Square, flanked by the Basilica Doges Palace and Bridge of Sigh

 ​​ Visit a Glass Blowing factory and observe how wonderful objects are blown from heated glass with clinical expertise

Day 4

Milan Shopping Day

 Serravalle Designer Outlets

 ​Spend a few hours at one of Italy’s best-known shopping outlets under the MacArthur’s Glen banner and enjoy savings up to 70%! Check out some Italy’s high fashion biggest names such as Prada, Ferragamo, Versace, Armani, Furla and a host of other fashionable brands.

 ​Return to Hotel Milan

Transfer by train to Zermatt

Visit Zermatt town

Overnight in Zermatt

Day 5

Milan to Switzerland

Day 6

Zermatt Day trip 

 ​ take the earliest cog railway (Gornergrat Bahn) to Riffelberg, after a short distance of walking to Riffelsee (2,757 m)

 ​ ​ The reflection of the Matterhorn during sunrise on the surface of the water is one of nature’s finest spectacles.

 ​ ​ ​Continue with the cog railway to the end station - Gornergrat(3,135 m) and enjoy the spectacular view of a total of 29 mountains together with the Gornergrat glacier

 ​ ​ ​​Overnight in Zermatt

Day 7

Visit Bern

 ​ Transfer to the regional train to get to Bern Bern City Tour (Switzerland) Wander around the UNESCO World Heritage Site Old Town byRiver Aare

 ​ ​Browse part of the 6km covered shopping arcade,view Zytglogge Clock Tower, take in the panoramic view of Old Town from Rosengarten and warm up to the lovely bears in Bear.

 ​ ​Overnight in Bern

Day 8

Bern - Interlaken

 ​One of the most beautiful places in Switzerland.Interlaken is the gateway to the Jungfrau Region and the ideal base for exploring the Lauterbrunnen Valley

 ​​After strolling around Lauterbrunnen village, we headed to Staubbach Falls, the most famous attraction here, and watched the waterfalls flowing down.

 ​​​Interlaken Shopping

 ​ ​Overnight in Bern

Day 9

Bern - ​Lucerne - Zurich airport

 ​​​Transfer to the regional train to get to Lucern before we heading to Zurich aiport

 ​​​​Visit to Chapel Bridge - the most iconic attraction in Lucerne

Day 10

Arrived KLIA/Changi airport
